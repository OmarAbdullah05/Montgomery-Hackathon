ADHD Test

This is a two-part test that will determine whether or not you have ADHD. Please note that this will not provide definitive results, it will simply help you identify aspects of your life that are associated with ADHD. The first part of this test will ask 15 questions. The second part will test your ability to focus. In the end, you will be provided with results. Good Luck!
